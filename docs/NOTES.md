# Notes

## Get the option values of a field

```sh
$ jira field getoptions my-jira-project__application | jq '.values | .[] | .value'
...
$ jira field getoptions my-jira-project__incident | jq '.values | .[] | .value'
...
$ jira field getoptions my-jira-project__typeIncident | jq '.values | .[] | .value'
...
$ jira field getoptions my-jira-project__produit | jq '.values | .[] | .value'
...
$ jira field getoptions my-jira-project__sousProduit | jq '.values | .[] | .value'
...
```
